Product Description

Our Organic Bananas are grown in the lush, tropical climates of South America, ensuring they develop the perfect balance of sweetness and creamy texture. Harvested at the optimal time, these bananas are allowed to ripen naturally, providing you with a delicious and nutritious fruit that's free from harmful chemicals.

Bananas are an incredibly versatile fruit. They come in their own natural, convenient packaging, making them the ultimate snack on the go. Mash them into banana bread, slice them over oatmeal, or freeze them for a creamy smoothie base.

Storage Tips

Store at room temperature away from direct sunlight. To speed up ripening, place them in a paper bag with an apple. Once ripe, you can store them in the refrigerator to extend their shelf life (the skin may brown, but the fruit inside will remain fresh).
