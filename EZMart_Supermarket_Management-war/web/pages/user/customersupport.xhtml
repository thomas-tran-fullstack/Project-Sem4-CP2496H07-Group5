Common Issues & Solutions

How do I track my order? expand_more

You can track your order in real-time by visiting the "Orders" section in your account. Enter your order number or check the status from your order history. We also send updates via email and SMS.

What's your return policy? expand_more

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on most items. To initiate a return, visit your order details and click "Return Item". Items must be in original condition with all packaging. Fresh produce cannot be returned.

How do I reset my password? expand_more

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email address and we'll send you a reset link. Follow the link to create a new password. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder.

What payment methods do you accept? expand_more

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), debit cards, PayPal, and digital wallets like Apple Pay and Google Pay. All transactions are encrypted and secure.

Is delivery available in my area? expand_more

We deliver to most areas within 25 miles of our store locations. Enter your ZIP code at checkout to check delivery availability. Some rural areas may take 1-2 business days for delivery.
